About Me

Located in Denver, Colorado, I'm originally from the San Francsico Bay Area. I have always been interested in technology, learning, and how things come together. I studied architecture to satisfy how different components of a building come together to create the places we inhabit. After beginning my professional career in architecture, I went back to school to learn the basics around business, how one might go about starting their own, and found an interest in marketing. Through marketing I've learned how to tell a story with impact and to reach a specific audience.
After much time in the architectural industry, I found myself seeking something else, something different to contribute to but that allowed me to learn how something came together. With an interest in technology, and feeling inspired by a friend's similar journey, I decided to learn about web development and am working on completing the Full Stack Web Development program through CareerFoundry.
With two young boys under three years old, my focus is continuing to learn web development with the goal of stability. I am curious, proactive, creative, devoted, and dependable. Organization and communication are paramount amongst my key skills. I prefer to assess and execute a plan rather than to react immediately and believe more is achieved through collaboration. I am able to work effectively as part of a team or on my own if needed.
